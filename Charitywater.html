When charity: water began, we made a bold promise: 100% of public donations would go directly to fund sustainable clean water projects. We’d even pay back credit card fees, meaning if a donor gave $100 with a credit card and we got only $97, we’d make up the $3 and send the full $100 to provide clean water for people in need.

So, how do we pay our operating expenses?

The 100% Model is powered by a small and dedicated group of private donors, known as The Well. From day one, we’ve relied on The Well to fund all of our operating expenses. It’s not always easy, but it’s an approach we believe in deeply. And year after year, our commitment to transparency inspires support from passionate donors, enthusiastic fundraisers, long-standing brand partners, and loyal volunteers who believe—like we do—that clean water truly changes everything.
